# 📊 Executive Overview Dashboard – Power BI

![Executive Overview Dashboard](executive_dashboard.png)

## 📌 Project Overview
The **Executive Overview Dashboard** is an interactive Power BI dashboard designed to provide key business insights for decision-makers. It enables executives and stakeholders to monitor sales performance, profitability, customer growth, and regional performance through visually rich and dynamic reports.

This dashboard transforms raw sales data into meaningful insights, helping organizations make data-driven decisions efficiently.

---

## 🎯 Objectives
- Analyze overall business performance.
- Monitor sales and profit trends over time.
- Compare sales across categories and regions.
- Track customer growth and order volume.
- Evaluate profit margins across different regions.

## 📊 Dashboard Visualizations

### 1️⃣ Total Sales by Order Date
- Displays sales trends over time.
- Helps identify seasonal patterns and growth trends.

### 2️⃣ Total Sales by Category (Donut Chart)
Categories analyzed:
- Technology
- Furniture
- Office Supplies

Provides a quick comparison of category-wise contribution to total sales.

### 3️⃣ Profit Margin % by Region (Pie Chart)
Regions analyzed:
- West
- East
- South
- Central

Helps identify high-performing and low-performing regions based on profitability.

### 4️⃣ Total Sales by Region (Bar Chart)
Compares sales performance across regions and highlights the best-performing markets.
